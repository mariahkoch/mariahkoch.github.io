I'M A

Printmaker

Printmaking is my favorite medium for studio work. There's something about hand-carving and physically making pieces that appeals to me. Most of the prints are in Black and White, however, my most successful prints are in color. The linework is very distinct and consistent throughout all of the pieces, as well as electric shapes and compositions. I hope to continue to create prints in my spare time after college.
